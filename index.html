Estéban Gabory

I am currently a postdoc at the University of Palermo. My research is generally on strings, and I am interested in both the algorithmic and combinatorial aspects of stringology.

One of my main interests lies in strings with elasticity, namely data structures that represent multiple similar strings. These are motivated by bioinformatics, and in particular, pangenomics.

Another line of research I am working on is the study of the structural properties of the Burrows–Wheeler Transform (BWT), its links with de Bruijn words, and abstract algebra.

Finally, I have some ongoing projects on consensus problems, from the perspective of parameterized complexity.
