I am currently a postdoc at the University of Palermo. My research is generally on strings, and I am interested in both the algorithmic and the combinatorial aspect of stringology.

One of my main interests lies in strings with elasticity , namely data structures that respresents multiple similar strings. They are motivated by bioinformatics, and, in particular, pangenomics

Another line of research I am working on is the study of the structural properties of BWT, its link with de Bruijn words, and abstract algebra.

Finally, I have some ongoing projects on consensus problems, in the angle of parameterized complexity
